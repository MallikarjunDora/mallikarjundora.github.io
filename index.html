I am an Assistant Librarian at the Vikram Sarabhai Library, Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad (IIM-A), where I have served since 2008. I am currently pursuing a PhD in Library and Information Science at Sardar Patel University, Gujarat, with a dissertation titled Evolution of Library and Information Science Research in India.

My research examines how Library and Information Science as a discipline evolves — nationally and internationally — with a focus on research trends, bibliometrics, scholarly communication, and the dynamics of academic publishing. I hold a Master of Library and Information Science from Sambalpur University, Odisha.

I have been an active member of IFLA and ASIS&T, and have served as Steering Committee Member for the IFLA RSCVD Group (2023–25) and Newsletter & Social Media Officer for ASIS&T South Asia (2022–23). Email is the best way to reach me!
