About

A decade of design,
rooted in intention.

I'm Alex — a Brooklyn-based creative director with ten years of experience building brands that feel genuinely alive. My process is slow, deliberate, and deeply collaborative. I believe the best design is almost invisible: it simply feels right.

Before going independent, I led visual teams at Condé Nast and a handful of beloved independent studios. These days I take on a small number of projects each year — the kind that deserve real attention.
