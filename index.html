About

Two decades of dedication to library science.

I am an Assistant Librarian at the Vikram Sarabhai Library, Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad (IIM-A), where I have served since 2008 in various capacities. My work sits at the intersection of library practice and scholarly research — I care deeply about how knowledge is organized, accessed, and shared.

My current research, pursued through a PhD at Sardar Patel University, examines the evolution of Library and Information Science research in India — tracking how the discipline grows, shifts, and contributes to global scholarship. Beyond academia, I am actively involved in international professional bodies including IFLA and ASIS&T.
